soundvizual, 2020

Soundvizual is a sound visualization that explores the relationship between sound and image, and attempts to find out how the combination of the two elements affects people.

Two human senses–sight and hearing–were combined to create a music visualization. It takes the viewer out of reality into a world created by the artist and the musicians and offers an audiovisual experience. The form of presentation played a major role, through the size and emptiness of the space, a feeling of being a part of the visualization was evoked in the observers.

Music

The music piece that serves as the basis of the work is Savanna Tripping, which was created by Ganaël Dumreicher, in collaboration with Lisa Hollaus and Vinzenz Landl.

musikvizual

Musikvizual is a collection of sound visualisations, created mainly with vvvv, it was created as part of the soundvizual project. An Instagram account (@soundvizal) was created to experiment, try out and learn new things with the combination of music and images. New videos were posted regularly to this account.

The goal of the project was to convey the feelings evoked by the music and to tell stories. Musikvizual was a part of my bachelor project.
