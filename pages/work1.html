Sound Campus design, 2023

Since 2020, Sound Campus is a format hosted by the University of Arts Linz for students and performers to exchange and share with a wider audience. A series of performances, open sessions, radio broadcasts, workshops and itinerant installations ttok place around Hauptplatz 6 during the Ars Electronica Festival.

For the visual language, a generative system was created with vvvv, which was used to create different images using the same graphics by changing the movement. This way the design had a uniform look with variation for every usage. It was used for moving and static images, in print and digital as well.

Digital usage

Instagram was the main tool for communication with the audience, so it was very important to create a straightforward feed, which would make it easy to find the relevant information.

For the announcements, such as daily programmes and lists of the artists, I decided to create static images. The cathegories were indicated with a different usage of the four main color and with variations in typography.

Instagram

An instagram post was created for every performing artist. The main idea was to find a way to show the artists and their works in one post. I decided to create a short video with audio to showcase the performances. Every video contained an image of the artist, an individual moving background and an audio snippet.

Analog usage

3 different kinds of posters were created and printed for the festival, which were serving with information about the artists who were participating the festival.
