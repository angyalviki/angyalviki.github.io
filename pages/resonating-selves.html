Resonating Selves

Brochure, map, banner, 2023

The design for the Interface Cultures exhibition at the Ars Electronica Festival 2023 was created in collaboration with Sofia Talanti. Sofia created the visual identity for the exhibition.

My task was to create the design for the banner and for the broschure containing the exhibited works with the general description and a map of the festival locations, while taking Sofia's design guidelines into account.
